Wind Analysis

There is not significant correlation between wind speed and nearness to the equator. The majority of cities sampled reported wind speeds of between 0 to 10 (mph). Interestingly, cities located further from the equator, particularly in the northern hemisphere, have greater likelihood of experiencing greater wind speeds (in excess of 15(mph)).
