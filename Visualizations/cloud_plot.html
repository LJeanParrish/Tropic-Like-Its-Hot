Cloudiness Analysis

Nearness to the equator does not impact the cloudiness a city experiences. The sample reflected that city cloudiness tended to concentrate at 0%, 75%, and 95%. This implies that cloudiness could be impacted by other attributes such as potential seasonal differences between hemispheres city elevation.
